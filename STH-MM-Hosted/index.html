How It Work

Simplified 4-steps process for trading.

  • Step 1

    Plan Your Trade.

    Before you buy or sell anything, understand the market you're entering. This could involve studying companies, economic indicators, or depending on what you're trading.

  • Step 2

    Set Up Your Trade.

    Open an account with a reputable online brokerage that allows you to trade your chosen asset class. Deposit money into your brokerage account to cover your initial investment and potential fees.

  • Step 3

    Execute Your Trade.

    Use your research and strategy to identify a trade opportunity. This might involve technical analysis (looking at charts) or fundamental analysis (focusing on company financials).

  • Step 4

    Manage Your Trade.

    Set a price to automatically sell if the market moves against you, limiting potential losses. Keep an eye on your investments and be prepared to adjust your strategy as needed.

What Is Trading ?

The buying and selling of financial assets in markets with the aim of making a profit. It involves analysing market trends and identifying opportunities to enter the market, thereby making a profit.

  • Few points about the Stock Market.

    The stock market involves buying and selling shares and derivatives (instruments whose value correlates in some way to particular stocks) of publicly traded companies. Investing in stocks means buying a piece of ownership in a company.

  • What is IPO and ETF ?

    An IPO ETF is an exchange-traded fund which tracks stocks that have recently conducted an initial public offering (IPO). This type of ETF is popular with day traders as it offers increased exposure to companies early on in their IPO, which allows them to take advantage of any price swings.
